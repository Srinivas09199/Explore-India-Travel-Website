Goa

The Arabian Sea on the western coast of India, Goa is a tropical paradise that captivates visitors with its sun-soaked beaches, lively culture, and a unique blend of colonial history and modern vibrancy. Renowned for its golden sands, pulsating nightlife, and welcoming locals, Goa stands as a destination that offers both relaxation and exhilarating experiences.

cost:25k- 50k

Book now

Manali

Perched in the lap of the mighty Himalayas, Manali, in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, is a captivating destination that seamlessly blends natural beauty, adventure, and a tranquil mountain ambiance. Renowned for its snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and vibrant local culture, Manali beckons travelers to experience the best of both adrenaline-pumping activities and serene retreats.

cost:30k- 60k

Book now

Alleppey

The serene backwaters of Kerala, Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is a mesmerizing destination that effortlessly captures the essence of tranquility, culture, and the unique beauty of the South Indian landscape. Renowned for its intricate network of canals, houseboats, and vibrant local life, Alleppey offers a rejuvenating escape for travelers seeking an authentic Kerala experience.

cost:25k- 30k

Book now

Coorg

The lush hills and valleys of the Western Ghats in Karnataka, Coorg, also known as Kodagu, stands as a captivating haven for nature enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and those yearning for a tranquil escape. This picturesque destination is renowned for its coffee plantations, mist-covered landscapes, and a rich tapestry of biodiversity.

cost:25k- 35k

Book now

Havelock Island

The azure waters of the Bay of Bengal, Havelock Island is a paradisiacal retreat that beckons travelers with its pristine white sandy beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant coral reefs. Part of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Havelock captivates visitors with its natural beauty and offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

cost:30k- 50k

Book now

Auli

Nestled in the majestic Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, Auli stands as a pristine gem, captivating visitors with its snow-covered slopes, panoramic mountain vistas, and a tranquil mountain atmosphere. Known as one of India's premier skiing destinations, Auli seamlessly combines adventure, natural beauty, and serene landscapes.

cost:25k- 50k

Book now
